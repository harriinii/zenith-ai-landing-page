Solving the Fragmented Placement Crisis

Personalized Placement Officers for Every Student.

India produces millions of graduates annually, yet personalized career support remains unscalable. Zenith.ai leverages LLMs to automate resume analysis, interview training, and job matching at scale.

The Problem

Millions struggle with resume building and job discovery. Colleges lack the bandwidth to provide 1:1 mentorship to every single student, leading to missed opportunities.

The Zenith Solution

An AI Placement Agent that acts as a 24/7 personalized officer—providing instant resume feedback, mock interview labs, and direct engine-led job matching.

The Tech-Stack Powering Placements

Built with FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and LLM APIs to deliver a high-performance career ecosystem.
